AI with Robotics – The Deadliest Combination


Debanjan Acharya

Artificial Intelligence and Robotics together will change the entire world because it is one of the most powerful combinations. The things which we can only imagine 10 years back are now becoming reality. In today's world AI and ROBOTICS are serving people as a problem solver ,fastest responders and companions. The adaptability of ROBOTICS and AI has made them the best and favourable in all sectors. For every field that is gaming ,education, fitness ,manufacturing and defence technology it is used. Many online gambling sites like TRUE BLUE CASINO have stated to have used AI based algorithms to control the games.

So it is really evident that the two will positively change the entire world in our recent futures.


USE OF AI AND ROBOTICS AS A VIRTUAL ASSISTANCE

The AI system in recent days has gained a lot of its glory with the help of robotics. Both technologies work at its best when combined together. Virtual assistance and chatbox use automated rules using capabilities like NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING (NPL). The recent advancements in the technologies have significantly improved their performance.In recent times the glorified versions of the VIRTUAL ASSISTANCE are SIRI to GOOGLE ASSISTANCE and ALEXA. They answer almost all the questions. With the help of IOT we can command our VIRTUAL ASSISTANCE to turn on AC, Light,Fan.


ADVANCED SECURITY

ROBOTICS and AI strongly have the most powerful use on Security and Surveillance and they use artificial intelligence , high definition (HD) cameras , long range and fast computing processes all of which marks the SECURITY SYSTEM for different needs. The recent AI SYSTEM uses FACE RECOGNITION to store the identities of people visiting to a particular place and creates a catalog of visitors who visited regularly and can remember the faces which are known.Some robots are designed with advanced GPS technology. Ao that they can find any objects easily. The foundation of AI based security systems is a self monitoring system that features HD VISUALIZATION. They also use FINGERprint and EYEBALL scanners which are mainly used in MILITARY HEADQUARTERS and many confidential places. They are mainly used to avoid the interference of any third party.


ARIEL TECHNOLOGY AND AUTONOMOUS FLYING

Autonomous flying is used in drones basically and COMPUTER VISION TECHNOLOGY (CVT) is used while avoiding obstacles on moving on a straight path. They are used in the defence force in any rescue missions etc. The main application of computer vision in flying includes obstacle detection , self navigation , object tracking etc. The drones in recent days are used in smart cities and management for advanced surveillance, recognition of certain stuff and tracing unwanted objects.It uses UAVs to capture real time data , it also uses an onboard intelligence system which makes it human independent. It is also used by armies to bombard or combat enemies in scanning or mapping terrains and buildings during attacks and surgical strikes.


GAMING

AI SYSTEM and ROBOTICS helps a lot in developing games and it is basically the main weapon of game developers.AI system helps the gamers to create a character that can imitate real human beings. Games are basically meant for entertainment and it also collects and processes data obtained from users. It helps games to create games based on their needs and expectations . AI based games have tremendous and stunning graphics .It actually needs more than a hundred developers to create a game but it is possible with an AI system. It saves our time and resources as well.


MANUFACTURING INDUSTRIES

Nowadays AI and ROBOTICS are also serving in the manufacturing industries.The main reason for its working in such industries is to cover the lack of workforce,improve efficiency and simplify the whole production process . It basically uses the teams effort to manage the task system but nowadays it has helped manufacturer to boosts their production speed.The main function of ROBOTICS and AI based system in these industries are DEMAND BASED PRODUCTION,AUTOMATIC CONTROL,DAMAGE CONTROL AND QUICK MAINTENANCE,PRODUCT DESIGNING AND REDESIGNING.


CONCLUSION

AI and ROBOTICS are the driving force of the technology of the future.AI is actually all about data but will use the given data for our benefits. By the end of the next decade ,we will surely see some stunning technologies based on AI and ROBOTICS.
