Impact Of Modern Technology In Current Covid Situation


Tanuka Mondal

From 2020 the whole world is fighting the dreadful virus COVID-19. The first thing is we have to maintain covid protocols, social distancing and on the other hand we can’t stop our daily work. We can’t compromise with our learning process. And modern technology has made things easier for us. I will discuss some of the cases where modern technology has a great impact on the new normal situation.

Online Education System:

Before covid situation, we all were used to going to school, college etc, for our education. But now more than a year we are continuing our learning process from our home. And that is possible only for the modern technology. We are using some devices like laptops, smartphones and many online platforms like zoom, google meet etc. We are even doing our practical work online using different softwares.

Work from home:

Not only students, most of the employees of IT sectors are doing their work from their home. In this situation we come to know that to do any work place doesn’t matter but technology matters.

Online business:

Online business is not any new thing, but this situation is the best time to grow online business. As most of the time people are quarantined in their home to maintain social distancing and avoid gathering, they are doing online marketing. There are many online shopping apps, and in covid situation many people have started using those apps. Online business is totally dependent on modern technology. Not only shopping, the number of online transactions is increasing day by day. Many people are using online methods to pay their electricity bill, recharge their phone and do a lot of work. The main point is day by day people are being dependent on modern technology.

Innovation to challenge covid-19:

A.Automatic sanitizer dispensing machine:
This machine is used to sanitize any place without any help of humans. In some places there are machines to sanitize the human body or hand automatically.
B. Use of robotics:
In some hospitals robots are used to deliver food and other work, as humans can be affected if they come in touch of covid patients. Asimov Robotics in Kerala has developed robots for this purpose.
C. Use of apps:
Software developers have developed so many apps to handle this situation. These apps are used for sharing covid protocols, treatment guidelines, information about medicines, beds, oxygen, vaccines etc.

Digitalization process was started before covid situation, but in this situation the needs of digitalization have increased very much. But as many citizens of India are not comfortable with online methods, we need more time to do all the things online.
