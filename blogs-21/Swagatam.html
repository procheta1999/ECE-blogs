ROLE OF TECHNOLOGY TO COMBAT COVID-19


Swagatam Adhya

As the COVID-19 cases continue to rise across our planet,every sector of the infrastructure is working hard to develop innovative solutions to fight the coronavirus pandemic.Pandemics,Epidemics and endemic had been threatening the homo sapiens since the dawn of human race.We have faced unexpected diseases such as SARS,Ebola,H1NI which have shown their outrage and have potentially destroyed the lives of millions of people.As a Engineering student it is my prime responsibility to bring to light the boon of converging technology such as cloud computing,Machine Learning,Artificial Intelligence,Robotics and high speed 5G internet connection that are the best innovative response to combat the COVID-19 situation.Since the beginning of 2020 the Covid-19 virus has brought the world to a standstill but we are also finding out many innovative ways to combat the situation so that we all can return back to our normal lives once again.

Wrong information about diagnosis,treatment techniques,vaccines,medicines and other various medical regulations creates more confusion and panic situation among the people.Social media platforms such as Google,Facebook and Youtube are tirelessly guiding people so that they get the right,proper and verified informations from accurate sources such as WHO(World Health Organization) or local authorities.The benefit of getting the right information plays an important role in creating a transparent situation in which people can get a clear idea about the current scenario and the treatment options.

Drones and robots can help to deliver essential goods and accessories avoiding human contact.We can use Drones to deliver test kits,medicines and sanitization products.Autonomous vehicles help to transport COVID infected people to quarantine zones without human intervention which can help to reduce the number of daily cases.Drones equipped with thermal scanning technologies can identify huge crowds and gatherings and help to reduce the rapid spread of the virus.Robots can be used for sterilizing hospitals,delivering food to the infected people.They can also be used for sample testing and vaccine production.Thus robots and automation technologies can help solve many problems without risking the lives of many people.

Artificial Intelligence based data analytics is becoming an essential part of the healthcare system to combat Covid-19 situation.Face Recognition technology help a lot in accurately identifying people to find out whether they are wearing mask or not.Risk assessment and forecasting can also be done through Artificial Technology for predictive modelling are helping medical professionals a lot to know more about the disease and to discover more efficient methods to combat the current situation.Many Artificial Intelligence based tools are efficient in differentiating the infected people from the non-infected people.

As the number of Cocid-19 cases are increasing the business world is taking the support of new technologies that conducts virtual meeting,cloud conferencing and augmented reality to make work from home more comfortable and efficient.Remote working is the best step to reduce the number of daily cases and maintain social distancing among people.

With the increase of the number of Covid-19 cases, technologies ranging from AI to robotics,the arena of innovations is helping us to manage the pandemic situation and help us to be more defensive against such viruses in future.
