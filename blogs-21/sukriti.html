OTP stands for one-time password, also called a one-time-pin or dynamic password. The invention of the one-time pad is generally credited to Gilbert S. Vernam and Joseph O. Mauborgne. TOTP stands for Time-based One Time Password. The moving factor in TOTP is time-based not counter based. In TOTP passwords valid for 30-60 seconds, that is called time-step. Time-step means the amount of time till the password is valid. If time limit exceed then you have to send the request to send the new TOTP.
TOTP Algorithm developed by IETF (Internet Engineering Task Force).

Which is better - HOTP or TOTP ?

TOTP is more popular than HOTP. Because the generate passcode is temporary and valid for 30-60 seconds , that provides higher security level. So, TOTP is better than HOTP.
