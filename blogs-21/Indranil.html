Why is Content writing important?Should we consider it as a domain for internship in our initial college years.


Indranil Saha Roy

What is branding?

Branding can be defined as a way in which you convince the customers to look at your business in a certain way which is usually a lot more than just colourful logs and catchy taglines. Logos , colours and taglines as important as they are do not entirely constitute a brand. The definition of the brand comes with its meaning and interpretation. And words are the best way to express that.


Why can content writing be considered important in branding?

Content writing is very important when it comes to tell people what your brand is all about . A good , consistent and high quality content impacts the audience more than any other way of branding, a cohesive and well written content will ensure that the reader understands what you want to express to your customers and this will further ensure the success and determine the wellbeing of your business.

Nowadays advertising has shifted from old-school means to digital advertising over social media platforms and by recommendation of a third party and hence it becomes all the more important to accompany your brand name with a cohesive and well written content which will be able to resonate with the reader.


The importance of content:

Elevating your brand: as mentioned earlier , catchy taglines and colourful logos are not enough to make your brand resonate with your audience. In today's time of digital advertising a cohesive and well written content will help to create an impact on the audience and hence will also help in growth of the brand as a whole.

Increased audience retention: a well written content will always grab your audience’s attention and once that i done it will attract customers to your brand, content writing when done correctly and consistently will provide satisfactory to your audience and hence will make them come back again and again

Reinforcing brand message: the best way to make your brand message clear to the audience and reinforcing it on them would be through a well written piece of article, once the reader reads it it will grab their interest and also make it easier for them to have a clear image of what the brand can do for them.

Increasing your presence on social media: most of the advertisement nowadays is done digitally through social media platform and an increased social media presence would be beneficial for the brand as a whole, in today's world of business an important consideration should be that of social media and while social media followers are an important aspect a beautifully written content will help you have some more

Your own voice: the best way to express what your brand stands for would be through your own voice, content writing here is important because it lets you express what your brand stands for, what it strives to achieve and what it promises for the customers. Once you have beautifully expressed yourself in the form of a content the reader or the customer understands it best and hence will be attracted to your brand.


Conclusion:

A well written content can be considered as a form of currency in today’s world . digital marketing in today's world in nothing but a constant stream of content and social actions and this is why blogging , creating video content and influencer marketing has emerged popular means of marketing nowadays A consistent and cohesive piece of text describing our goals and aims can be considered as something we can use to get the audience to take an interest in. This interest can in turn be used to ensure a greater sale of our product. And meanwhile the more the content is shared the more sales it will bring. Hence content writing is important and is indeed a way to create a conversation between you and the customer.

Now once we have established how important content writing is , Lets have a look how it can actually be helpful for you.

Firstly , it doent really require much technical knowledge, all you need is basic english writing proficiency and the skill to research on a topic. Hence it provides a great opportunity for us to concentrate on other aspects of an internship/job like regularity and meeting deadlines.

It also provides an opportunity to help a student develop on their english proficiency which will be nothing but useful in the near future.

Lastly it provides a great source for extra Income, Honestly who doesnt love their wallets to be a liitle more thick.
