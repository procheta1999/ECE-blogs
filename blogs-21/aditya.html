IoB (Internet of Behavior)


Aditya Banerjee

Thanks to IoT that nowadays humans are a click away from almost every action or information in the world. Introduction of smartphones and good internet connectivity has helped more and more people to connect with the world around them, know other people and has opened a variety of new job options. IoT has a very important role to play in our day to day lives for example smart speakers like google home or Alexa, home security systems , smartlocks and many more. The IoT device basically collects valuable information about the users for example their behavior, preferences , interests and then shows suitable results. This has led to the introduction of a new technology known as Internet of Behaviour or IoB. The Covid 19 pandemic has brought about s huge change around the world, especially in the way people communicate . That is why IoB has become one the latest trends of 2021, as it helps people communicate with the companies during these situations, as a result of which most companies are looking forward to IoB.

IoB is an extension of IoT which mainly focuses on people's behavior. It gives vast data and information about user's behavior, preferences and interests which helps optimizing the user's search experience.

According to Gartner, IoB combines technologies focused on tracking location and facial recognition of the people, connecting the data, and mapping them to behavioral events. IoB is a process where user data is analyzed in terms of behavioral psychology. Based on this analysis, new approaches to the development of user experience (UX), search experience optimization (SXO), and end products and companies' services and how to promote them are formed. Implementing IoB is pretty simple as it requires statistical studies like mapping everyday behavior of users following the privacy agreement for legal reasons. IoB helps in analyzing human behaviour and accordingly improvises the search results so that the person can get better and accurate results everytime he wants something. In this pandemic situation since everything is becoming online, people tend to shop online, work online even open new companies online, that's why IoB plays a major role in today's world. It helps promote these companies or suggests a product for shopping depending on the user's behavior. This results in better user experience and also accurate promotion of the companies or product. The main of objective of IoB is to improve efficiency and quality of the system . IoB mainly comprises of technology , data analytics and behavorial science. It guides the customer throughout the process of suggesting a suitable product according to his needs to buying it and delivering it.

A basic example of IoB would be a heath app like healthify me which tracks your day to day activities and calorie intake and suggests you what should be done to make you more healthy. It shows how the app analyses the behavior of the user and accordingly shows the exact information that the user needs to improve his helath.
