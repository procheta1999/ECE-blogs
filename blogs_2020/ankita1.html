The Future of IOT


Ankita Hazra

It seems like everybody is talking about the internet of things (IoT) whether it is good or bad. The IoT is when everyday products like refrigerator, speakers, watches and more are connected to the internet and to one another. The Internet of things are already transforming our homes and workplaces. So, now the question arises what is the future of this Internet of things??

Wave of the future

In the future, there will be so many IP addresses, so many devices, sensors, things that we are wearing, things that we are interacting with that we don't even sense it. It will be a part of our presence all the time. Suppose imagine that we entered into a room that is dynamic and with all our permissions and all of that we can actually interact with the things going on in that room.

Major impact in the industry

IoT is transforming the everyday objects that surrounds us into an ecosystem of information that will enrich our lives. From speakers to parking spaces to our homes IoT is bringing a revolution everywhere. Smart homes and other connected products will not be aimed at home life only they are also going to have a major effect in the industrial growth too.

The new revolution

IoT is changing and transforming everything from business to life. Imaginations are boundless and opportunities are infinite. Everything is being wired up or connected wirelessly- architecture, energy efficient sensing, secure networks, cloud computing, IS based visualization and international activities. It means that the powerful information will be at our fingertips.

The global industrial sector is poised to undergo a fundamental structural change in the upcoming years. Equipment is becoming more and more digitized and connected establishing networks between machines, humans and the internet leading to the creation of higher productivity, better efficiency and more profitability.
